Offshore Software Development Outsourcing
The Agile Assurance Group is uniquely positioned to provide the quality and cost efficiencies inherent in outsourcing software development offshore to the Philippines. As small- and medium-sized entrepreneurial firms search for economical means for software development, Agile stands ready to deliver while addressing the concerns our clients have experienced in other Southeast Asian markets. Agile’s expatriate executive technology managers, hailing from North America, Europe and Australia, leverage their decades of experience from working in in the burgeoning information technology sectors throughout Southeast Asia, providing cost efficiencies unavailable to businesses in Europe and North America.
The cost overhead to maintain in-house a staff of quality developers, testers and Quality Assurance managers is often prohibitive, and the number of graduates with technology degrees in Europe and the United States lags on a per capita basis from those hailing from top-tier universities and technology firms in Manila.

The creativity, business good will, entrepreneurial focus and efficiencies of an American or European firm are inhibited by the soaring costs associated with in-house software development. Offshore software development outsourcing slashes development costs without dampening quality, allowing North American and European firms to focus on core efficiencies. Maintaining highly qualified staff in-house, or ramping up engineers, programmers, analysts, designers and testers for software development for individual projects, can be prohibitive and detract from the firm’s core business.

Agile, headquartered in Hong Kong with its main operation center in Makati, the technology and financial hub of Manila, Philippines, maintains an engineering staff that quickly assimilates a client’s unique business needs and works hand-in-hand throughout the Software Development Life Cycle. In Manila, the information technology sector has sprouted from the business process outsourcing (BPO) industry, where the education and English language skills of Filipinos have created the world’s largest call center hub, surpassing India and other emerging economies. While English is the second language spoken by the majority of Filipinos, it is the official language for education, business and education, negating any concerns of communication barriers Agile’s clients may have.

Independent Software Vendors have sprouted from this technology and cultural base, with universities and technical institutes training a skilled workforce with a strong work ethic bolstered by a stable democratic government bent on economic growth. Tax breaks for Regional Operating Headquarters administered by the Philippines Board of Investors enable Agile to pass along additional business savings to overseas clients.

When offshore software development outsourcing first emerged on the radar of North American and European companies, the initial benefit was the affordable salaries paid information technology workers in emerging economies. Firms availed themselves of mid-level positions in the scheme of software development while maintaining expensive project management in-house, a costly disadvantage at the time; but the talent in Southeast Asia’s technology labor pool has matured, often led by expatriate executive developers, and provides top-quality developers, programmers, testers and quality assurance staff who still work for emerging economy salaries.

Advantages to offshore software development outsourcing with Agile include lower labor costs, lower operating costs, lower labor training costs and less production downtime.

Less expensive labor costs for skilled engineers and technicians was often eroded in many emerging economies because of management overhead. Agile, on the other hand, well versed in local capabilities, culture, education and the business environment, combined with their decades of experience in the technology sectors of the United States, Ukraine and Australia, negate this potential pitfall.

The work culture evolving from the maturing call center or BPO sector in the Philippines eliminates the downtime often associated with offshore software development outsourcing. Makati, the Central Business District of the Philippines, hums 24 hours a day, with skilled labor working shifts to accommodate the time zones of clients in North America and Europe. (Manila is GMT +8.)

Operating costs may be the largest advantage of offshore software development outsourcing. Agile’s clients realize enormous savings in operational overhead, reducing expensive office space and computer network needs.

Disadvantages to offshore software development testing in other markets are huge, and include erosion of cost advantage, culture, quality, low management maturity, skill availability, and staff turnover. Addressing these potential risks has elevated Agile’s services to fill a badly needed niche.

In Makati, under the auspices of Agile’s expatriate executive management, tapping top-tier talent ensures the highest quality of service, picking seasoned staff from the cream of the crop of Independent Software Vendors throughout Southeast Asia and emerging talent from highly rated educational centers working under the supervision of local managers with strong project and program management experience. In the Philippines, dedicated staff committed not only to individual but organization success is culturally inherent, reducing turnover that can contribute to project down time. Skilled labor costs for technology staff has remained constant, unlike in other emerging economies.
