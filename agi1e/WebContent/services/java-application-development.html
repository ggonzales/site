Java Application Development
The Agile Assurance Group is a leading Java Application Development company with service delivery based in Manila, Philippines.
Agile’s strength in Java Development comes from its internationally experienced operations and project management teams, core focus on Quality Assurance, mature application of Agile Development (and testing) Methodologies, and of course – excellent Java developers. Although our development center is in Asia, we typically share some working hours with most of our clients – usually based in the Mid-Atlantic States and California.

Although all of our projects are unique, some of our more typical uses of Java are for eCommerce, social media & networking, medical, ERP, and financial applications.

Why use the Agile Assurance Group for your Java Application Development?

We are not the biggest development company in the world – or the smallest; but we pride ourselves on being among the best. We think the following is what sets us apart:

Process.
All of our approaches are tried and true, manageable, and replicable. We employ Agile Development and Testing Methods/Processes on virtually all of our projects. We do not seek to reinvent the wheel, nor do we stray from what is proven to work

Management.
Our development center is led by a multi-national team with decades of work on international projects. There is no substitute for management, even if you do have the world’s best developers.

QA Focus.
The Agile Assurance Group lives by the quote “If it’s worth building, it’s worth testing. If it’s not worth testing, why are you wasting your time working on it?” We will not accept a project that excludes provisions for testing.

Java Developers.
Our development teams are anchored with technical leads with no less than a decade of Java Application Development experience. They develop with and lead talented intermediate and junior level developers with suitable experience.

Dedication.
As an Offshore Java Application Development Company – smaller projects are impractical for us. We only develop applications that allow us to dedicate, at minimum, one full-time Java Developer for two months or more. None of our Java developers work on part-time projects. You receive total focus and dedication from the resources on your project without competing deadlines or project owners.

Communication.
Our projects thrive on constant communication with project owners. As an Agile Assurance Group client, you will be invited to daily standup sessions and weekly project management meetings. Of course, the bigger or more complex the project the more frequent the communication.

Colocation.
The development process is meant to be a collaborative one. All too often we have seen teams working different hours in different places. The result is virtually always efficiency loss and a less than manageable timelines and outcomes. The fact is – very few developers are mature enough to work this way. It is virtually impossible to locate and assemble a team that is capable of working remotely at optimal or even acceptable efficiency.
