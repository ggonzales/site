Agile’s core focus is Quality Assurance and our expatriate executive management, hailing from Australia, Europe and the United States, has worked on both sides of the offshore PHP application development services paradigm. We are versed in the advantages and potential pitfalls our clients have experienced with competitors, and lead an experienced staff of designers, PHP developers and testers who are apt in adjusting to the unique needs of our clients. Why turn to the Agile Assurance group for your PHP application development services?

Agile is uniquely positioned in terms of staff, company size, and experience to consistently provide quality PHP development services. Our clients have found the following principles reason to turn to Agile when going offshore for development and testing services:

Agile.
Agile is not just our name, it is what we do, applying the Agile Development Methodologies in all our practices. We do not eschew all other development practices – we will apply the best approach to the unique characteristics of each project.

Experienced management.
Our executive leaders have decades of combined experience in PHP application development throughout South East Asia. We have worked on both sides of the offshore development equation, and know the advantages and potential issues in operating from the Philippines.

Quality.
Quality Assurance is our hallmark; testing is our core service. Testing schemes are devised before the first line of code is written. We will not accept PHP application development projects that do not require testing as a core function.

Resources.
While we are a small Independent Software Vendor, our offshore clients must realize we do not tackle part-time projects. At a minimum, our PHP application development projects must require at least one full-time PHP developer for a project requiring a minimum of two-months from start to deployment. We do not juggle projects, but rather focus our resources from start to project conclusion.

Communication.
Our clients need to know where Agile stands on project iterations, and this requires – and we provide – daily progress reports on projects and issues. These daily stand-up meetings ensure the team, team leaders and project owners are always on the same page.

Colocation.
Experience has taught us that multiple teams working from multiple locations simply do not work. Out team of PHP developers work from our Manila operations center, sharing shifts that best coordinate with our clients’ normal business hours. Failing to follow the benefits of colocation of our resources results in missed deadlines, budget overruns and lowers quality. We don’t do it.
