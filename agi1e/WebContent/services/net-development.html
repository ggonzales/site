.Net Development
The Agile Assurance Group’s .NET development services, following the Agile Development Methodology, are led by Agile’s expatriate executive management and top-tier project managers, engineers, developers and testers recruited from the burgeoning technology sector in the Philippines.
Headquartered in Hong Kong with our main operations center in Manila, Agile’s core focus is on Quality Assurance and the mature application of the Agile philosophy inherent in our name.

While operations are located in Manila (GMT +8), our development center operates in shifts that match business hours in the United States, ensuring Agile’s commitment to communication throughout the ASP.NET development life cycle meshes with our client’s normal operational hours.

Each of our clients’ builds are unique, but our typical ASP.NET projects are for eCommerce, social media and networking, medical, enterprise resource planning (ERP), and financial applications.

Why use the Agile Assurance Group’s ASP.NET development services?
Agile methodology.
Agile follows the Agile Development and Testing Methods, following proven, manageable and replicable processes. Agile selects the best development methodology for the specific project. While we don’t reinvent the wheel for each project, we only ride on wheels we have found, tried and true.

Leadership.
Our executive management hails from Australia, the United States and Europe, while project management has been recruited from the technology, financial and Business Process Office sector in Manila. English is the standard language for business, education and government in the Philippines, erasing client concerns of communication when looking offshore for ASP.NET development services.

Quality Assurance.
The Agile Assurance Group adheres to a simple philosophy: Design testing before the first line of code is written. Testing is a core practice of all Agile does. We cannot accept project assignments that exclude testing throughout the build.

Experience.
Agile’s development leaders have a minimum of 10 years in ASP.NET web development and guide talented intermediate and junior-level ASP.NET developers with experience tailored for your ASP.NET web development project.

Commitment.
Agile Assurance Group is an offshore ASP.NET web development company, but smaller projects are unsuitable for us. Agile accepts projects with a minimum requirement of one ASP.NET developer for a project lasting two months or longer. Our staff cannot work on part-time projects. An Agile ASP.NET developer is assigned to your project fulltime, eliminating the distraction of juggling multiple .NET software development projects.

Transparency.
We know Agile’s clients need to know where a project stands at any given point, and practical, consistent communication – where our team is available to our clients when they need us, not when it is convenient – is a hallmark of all Agile does.

Colocation.
Team Collaboration is the foundation of the Agile Development Methodology, and it defines how Agile attacks any ASP.NET web development project. Our management has seen the fallacy of having team members working at different hours and different locations, and they fail in meeting deadlines, coming in on budget and releasing quality projects. Agile’s staff is in-house, in the same office, participating in the same daily meetings and available for efficiency, client communication and Quality Assurance in each incremental step of the development process.
