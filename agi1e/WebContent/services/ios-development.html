Why do our clients turn to the Aglile Assurance Group for iOS app development services?

Expatriate leadership.

Agile’s leaders understand the equation from both sides, having experience as clients and offshore providers.

Teams.
Agile has tapped the cream of the crop of designers, testers, developers – our engineering staff has been recruited from top-tier organizations in academia and the burgeoning technology sector in the Philippines. Methodology.
Agile is inherent in Agile’s name, and defines what we bring to every project as specified in the Agile Development Methodologies and testing practices. Our practices evolve from experience, and are manageable, replicable, and proven.

Workability.
Agile’s iOS application development services are validated through the Agile Development Methodologies, resulting in potentially shippable incremental products. Quality Assurance is our hallmark. Agile does not accept projects that exclude rigorous testing. Testing is inherent in all we do.

Availability.
Communication is the single most important quality in Agile’s methods, hinging in constant, consistent and quality communication among all stakeholders throughout the application development lifecycle. The project owner is the most important member of each project team.. We recognize communication is a major concern of clients searching for a quality offshore iOS development provider, and the single most critical component in following the Agile Development Methodologies.

Quality staff.
Agile’s developers have no less than a decade of build and testing experience, and our iOS developers have developed iOS applications since the operating system’s inception in 2007. Our iOS app development team leaders guide talented intermediate and junior developers with suitable experience.

Project control.
Colocation of all team members is mandatory at Agile. Agile’s executive management has seen he failures inherent in team members working from various locations over changing work schedules. It doesn’t work. The result is lowered quality, missed deadline, budget overruns and dissatisfied clients.

Commitment.
